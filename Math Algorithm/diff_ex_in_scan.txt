Inclusive Scan

Input

Index:  0   1   2   3
Value:  2   5   9   15

Output

Index:  0   1   2   3
Value:  2   7   16   31

Notice:

Output[0] = Input[0]

2

Automatically.

Exclusive Scan

Yahan STL poochta hai:

"Output[0] me kya daalna hai?"

Tum decide karte ho.

Example

std::exclusive_scan(
    v.begin(),
    v.end(),
    ans.begin(),
    0
);

Output

0 2 7 16

Agar likho

std::exclusive_scan(
    v.begin(),
    v.end(),
    ans.begin(),
    100
);

Toh

100 102 107 116
